Women's Events Survey

Kittyhawk is looking into adding additional events to the calender, and will appreciate your participation in the survey to get a feeling of what racers are looking for.

Additional race formats are dedicated series for climbing, time trialing, and criteriums (points races), and the chase race format. The chase race format is definitely one that Kittyhawk is quite interested in, as there is not a lot of opportunity for women in current chase races.

Kittyhawk is also looking for feedback on your current timezone, and times available for racing in, to achieve suitable event scheduling.

Finally, trying to gauge interest in how racers feel about fastest timed segments and first across the line bonus points, and whether racers are willing to trial racing without the HUD, in an effort to make racing more tactical.

So if you can spare a few minutes, please complete our survey, Kittyhawk thanks you very much for your input!
