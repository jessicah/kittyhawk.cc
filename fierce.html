Event Information

The Fierce Felines is a series by Kittyhawk Racing, providing hard racing for women across three timezones. We believe that women need more opportunities to race against women, and that races should be as equally hard as some of the top mens racing in Zwift. This series provides races that should cater to all abilities, with time trials, points races, fairly flat road races, and hilly road races, taking your best six results for the series.

The Finish Order Points Structure

These are the points that will be accumulated in the results table, and your best six results will contribute to your total series score.

There will also be a Sprint Queen, and Queen of the Mountains competition, where the fastest overall time for each unique sprint segment, and each unique climbing segment earn points. The points will be 10, 9, 8, ..., down to 1 point for the fastest 10 riders. This means that if you race through a given segment multiple times, only your fastest time will count. You will still need to finish the race to be eligible.

For the team's classification, it will take the best rider of each scored segment and the best placed rider at the finish, to contribute to your team's score.
