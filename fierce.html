Fierce Felines p/b Kittyhawk

Event Information

The Fierce Felines is a series by Kittyhawk Racing, providing hard racing for women across three timezones. We believe that women need more opportunities to race against women, and that races should be as equally hard as some of the top mens racing in Zwift. This series provides races that should cater to all abilities, with time trials, points races, fairly flat road races, and hilly road races.

Race Details

Week 1: Handful of Gravel

Week 1 starts off with a scratch race, no segments on this course, but bumpy enough to make racing difficult. The race will do four laps of this mostly gravel course.

Finish time determines finish order.

Powerups: Leaf, Anvil, and the Steamroller.

Week 2: Downtown Dolphin

Week 2 drops you into a points race, points will be awarded on the Downtown Dolphin QOM, and double points for the finish. For this event, categories will be separated, due to the extremely short nature of the course.

Downtown Dolphin QOM: first across the line: 10, 9, 8, ..., 3, 2, 1. (x15)

Finish time: 20, 19, 18, ..., 3, 2, 1.

Total awarded points determines finish order.

Week 3: Bologna TT

Pretty simple: grab your best TT rig, and settle in for a world of hurt.

Finish time determines finish order.

Week 4: Climber's Gambit

An epic stage to the top of the Epic QOM. There will be FTS bonus points on the intermediate sprint early in the stage, then FAL points for Titans Grove QOM, and double FAL points for the Epic QOM finish.

Epic QOM Reverse (finish): first across the line: 20, 19, 18, ..., 3, 2, 1.

Titans Grove QOM Reverse: first across the line: 10, 9, 8, ..., 3, 2, 1.

JWB Sprint Reverse: fastest timed segment: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.

Total awarded points determines finish order.

Week 5: Greatest London Flat

Back into scratch racing territory, with FTS bonus points on the intermediate sprint on the Mall Sprint (reverse).

Finish time determines points: 50, 49, 48, ..., 1. 1 point for the rest.

Mall Sprint Reverse on lead-in: fastest timed segment: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.

Total awarded points determines finish order.

Week 6: Chain Chomper

And back into the hills, two laps, with FAL points on Castle & Temple KOMs, and FTS bonus points for the Country Sprint.

Castle KOM: first across the line: 10, 9, 8, ..., 3, 2, 1. (x2)

Temple KOM: first across the line: 20, 19, 18, ..., 3, 2, 1. (x2)

Country Sprint: fastest timed segment: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1. (x2)

Total awarded points determines finish order.

Week 7: Sprinter's Playground

Another two laps, this time in the heart of Neokyo, eight sprints in total, and double points for the finish line.

All sprints: 10, 9, 8, ... 3, 2, 1.

Finish time: 20, 19, 18, ..., 3, 2, 1.

Total awarded points determines finish order.

Week 8: Triple Flat Loops

It's back to the TT rigs, but this time on the much flatter roads of Watopia to round out the series.

Finish time determines finish order.

The Finish Order Points Structure

These are the points that will be accumulated in the results table, and all eight weeks will contribute to your total. This may be changed to your best six results, if it makes the series more appealing.

There will also be a Sprint Queen, and Queen of the Mountains competition, where the fastest overall time for each unique sprint segment, and each unique climbing segment earn points. The points will be 10, 9, 8, ..., down to 1 point for the fastest 10 riders. This means that if you race through a given segment multiple times, only your fastest time will count. You will still need to finish the race to be eligible.

For the team's classification, it will take the best rider of each scored segment and the best placed rider at the finish, to contribute to your team's score.
